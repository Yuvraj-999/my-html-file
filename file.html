About PRIS

Learning By Doing

Priyadarshani International School is a progressive, Reggio-inspired institution in the Kadwa Valley. We aim to create a learning space where children believe "Anything Can Happen."

We focus on developing intelligence with character, combining academic rigour with social responsibility. We equip students to face future global challenges by fostering logical thinking and emphasising 21st-century skills.
